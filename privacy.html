Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2nd, 2026

Introduction

At Soma Studios, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when using our AI virtual try-on service.

Information We Collect

When using our service, you may upload two photos of yourself to see AI-generated images of how clothing items would look on you. These photos are never stored on our servers. Instead, they are sent directly to Google Gemini for processing. Once the AI generates the try-on images, your uploaded photos are immediately deleted. At no point are your photos used for any other purpose, including AI training or analytics.

Third-Party Processing

By using our service, you acknowledge and agree that your photos are processed by Google Gemini. This processing may occur outside of Canada, including in the United States. By submitting your photos, you also agree to Google Gemini's terms of service in addition to this Privacy Policy.

Security

All photos are transmitted securely using encrypted connections. We do not retain, share, or make your photos accessible to any third party other than Google Gemini for the sole purpose of generating your try-on images.

User Consent

By using our AI try-on feature, you confirm that the photos uploaded are of yourself and that you have the right to provide them for this purpose. You also consent to the processing of your photos in accordance with this Privacy Policy and Google Gemini's terms.

Data Retention

Since we do not store your photos, no additional action is needed to request deletion. Any generated images on your device can be deleted manually at your discretion.
